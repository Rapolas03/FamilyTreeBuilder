About This Tool

Family Tree Builder is a web-based application designed to help you create and visualize your family connections. Whether you're researching genealogy, preserving family history, or simply want to map out your relatives, this tool provides an easy-to-use interface for building comprehensive family trees.

No registration required. Start building immediately and organize your family history in a clear, visual format.
